About

CometAI represents a new approach to AI-assisted scientific software development. By fine-tuning state-of-the-art language models on domain-specific codebases, we create intelligent assistants that truly understand the physics and engineering behind the code.

Our initial focus is the COMET DSMC codebase, enabling researchers and developers to navigate complex particle simulation code, understand physical implementations, and accelerate their development workflow.
